Black Bean Chili

If you are looking for a cozy meal to prep for the week or just something to keep you warm when it is cold give this black bean chili a try!

This black bean chili is a nice and easy way to feed a crowd. Freezes well and easily doubled for a larger crowd.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 pound of ground turkey (optional)
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans of black beans, undrained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1.5 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes
  2. Add turkey;cook and stir until browned and crumbly,5 to 7 minutes
  3. Stir in beans,tomatoes, chili powder,oregano,basil,vinegar.Reduce heat to low,cover, and simmer until flavors are well blended, about 1 hour.
  4. Serve and enjoy! If you have leftovers you can freeze them!
